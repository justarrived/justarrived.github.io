Link builder (for Google Analytics)



There are a total of five parameters you can add to your URLs: utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, utm_term, utm_content.

For example, you could set the parameter utm_source as newsletter to identify traffic that come from a newsletter. You could then combine it with the parameter utm_campaign set to june to identify that this was the campaign in the month of June.
