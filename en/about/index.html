Yesterday, today and tomorrow

The foundation for Just Arrived was laid the autumn of 2015 with increasing numbers of refugees arriving to Sweden. In 2016 Just Arrived added staffing to our business and Just Arrived Bemanning AB was founded. Today we operate in Stockholm and Göteborg, looking forward to help employers and foregin-born candidates in more cities to meet.
