Welcome and
Happy New Year!

Thank you for helping us to lower the thresholds for newcomers to enter the Swedish labour market.

Today Sweden has thousands of newcomers who are driven and motivated to get a foot in the Swedish labour market. The challenges are many but we're determined to, together with you, make sure that many more get #enfotin.

We have at Just Arrived built a service that makes this process trivial. For each company that users the service more newcomers get #enfotin.

This is our December-challenge for you - helping newcomers to get #enfotin.

How it works



1. Registrer - You provide the name and contact details for you and your company.

2. Confirmation - You will get a thank you and your name will appear under the "Latest supporters" section on this page.

3. We'll be in touch - We will contact you in the beginning of next year, to talk more about how we together can help newcomers get #enfotin.



1. Take a photo - Take a picture of your foot (with shoes on), with your smartphone.

2. Post the photo to social media - Write a short text of why you think its important that newcomers get a chance to enter the Swedish labour market. Don't forget to use the hashtag #enfotin!

About Just Arrived

Just Arrived is a non-profit company and digital service that match newcomers and companies that need help. We provide the validation, matching and take care of all the paperwork so you don't have to administrate anything. Also we take full employer responsibilities.
