Get the Experience You Need to Enter the Swedish Labor Market

By matching you with Swedish companies Just Arrived helps you get the credentials you need to enter the Swedish labor market.

1. Fulfill Requirements

  • Work permit (or AT-UND)
  • Bank account
  • Personal id number or a coordination number

Apply

2. Apply

  • Register and fill out our form to apply to Just Arrived. This will take a couple of minutes and is open for anyone to do.
  • When and how you get contacted depends on; In what city you live, how many and what type of jobs we have available at the moment.
  • An interview will be conducted.

Apply

3. Enter the Labor Market

  • You get access to the Just Arrived service
  • Create your profile and start applying for jobs!
